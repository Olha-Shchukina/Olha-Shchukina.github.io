Photographer & Videographer

Olha Shchukina

With a background in Film Production from the Kyiv National University of Theatre, Cinema and Television, I began my creative journey through moving images. Now based in Madrid, I’m completing a photography course at EIMA, where my passion for still imagery has truly found its place.

My work is shaped by the language of cinematography and influenced by directors like Federico Fellini, Wes Anderson, and Alejandro González Iñárritu. I’m drawn to clean compositions, genuine human emotion, symbolism and an editorial touch — all woven together with a strong sense of storytelling.

Here you’ll find a selection of my photography works. I hope you enjoy them as much as I enjoyed creating them.
